# DGD3004_PrincessFate
Princess Fate GDD

Game Title: ”Princess Fate” 

Genre

2D Platformer with Action, Puzzle, and Horror Elements

Team Members & Which Level They Designed

Mete Eren Çelik(Game Designer) –> Level 1: “Forest”

Halil Ege Özer(Artist) –> Level 2: “Castle”

Ömer Ceylan(Game Developer) –> Level 3: “Basement”

Game Goal

The player starts in a 3D room with a computer and puzzle devices. By solving each puzzle, the player receives a disk that unlocks the next level on the computer.
The player’s goal is to move through three levels in a 2D platformer game. In each level, the player must pass platforms, fight enemies, and solve a puzzle at the end to unlock the next stage. The final aim is to reach the princess’s room in the last level.
The player thinks they are saving the princess, but in the end they learn that the guards were humans and the hero is the real monster. The game ends with the princess being killed, and it closes after a jumpscare.

Core Mechanics

Double Jump: By pressing the jump key two times, the player can reach higher places. 
Jump: The player can jump to pass platforms and avoid obstacles.
Attack: The player can fight enemies using basic attack.
Wall Jump: The player can jump from walls to move up.
Puzzle: At the end of each level, the player must solve a puzzle to continue.

Controls 

W –> Move “Forward”
A –> Move “Left”
S –> Move “Backwards”
D –> Move “Right”
E –> “interact”
R –> Restart after death
Space –> “Jump”
Space 2x –> “Double Jump”
Left Mouse Button –> “Attack” / “interact”

Target Audience & Platform

Princess Fate is made for players “13+” and who enjoy 2D platform games with action and puzzle elements. 
The game is developed for PC (Windows) and can be played with keyboard and mouse.
